TWO TALK

SMALL WORDS. BIG IDEAS.

Two Talk is a semantic micro-notation system designed for the speed of the digital age. While traditional shorthand systems require years of practice, Two Talk uses the universal symbols already on your keyboard to map out meaning rather than sounds. It's 60% faster than English, works across language barriers, and is built to help you capture the "essence" of a thought before it escapes your brain.

Key Features

  • Fixed Arity (The 2-Symbol Rule): Every "word" in Two Talk is exactly two symbols long - a Category and a Modifier.
  • Semantic Calculus: It translates concepts, not sounds. One pair can act as a noun, verb, or adjective depending on context.
  • Cognitive Categories: The 10 categories are based on how humans naturally "chunk" reality.
  • Compositional Chaining: Stack 2-symbol blocks for complex thoughts. The first block is the Subject, and following blocks provide Detail.

Advantages

  • Keyboard Ready: Every symbol is available on every smartphone and laptop on Earth.
  • Deductive Vocabulary: You don't need to memorize a massive dictionary; you can "invent" words based on logic.
  • Universal & Scannable: It bypasses language barriers and creates a visual rhythm that is easy to skim.
  • Ambiguity as a Feature: Relies on human logic to determine specific meaning from context.

Benefits

  • Fast and Efficient: Strip away grammatical noise to focus purely on data.
  • Easy to Learn: Most users become proficient in just hours.
  • Readable Later: Systematic structure keeps notes intelligible for years.
  • Systematically Expandable: Chaining allows for unlimited complexity.

Two Symbol Rule: Every word uses exactly two symbols. A "category" plus a "modifier".
