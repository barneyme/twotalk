Two Talk "Shorthand, Simplified."

What is Two Talk?

Two Talk simple way to write quickly. Each word uses only 2 symbols. The first symbol is the Subject (The Thing) and the second is the State (The Way it is).

Two symbols First symbol: What are we talking about? Second symbol: What is its current condition?
Three Choices Each pair can be a Thing (Noun), an Idea (Feeling), or an Action (Verb).
Make complex words by joining symbol pairs with a dot (.)
