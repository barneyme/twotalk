What is Two Talk?

Two Talk is a compact semantic shorthand system built for fast, structured notes, task lists, digital bullet journals, and personal logs. It turns everyday notes into tiny, consistent building blocks you can type at the speed of thought. Two Talk is great for knowledge workers, developers, and digital minimalists who prefer structure over prose. Each word uses only 2 symbols. The first symbol is the Subject (The Thing) and the second is the State (The Way it is).

Symbols

Two Talk words are created by combining a "subject" symbol and a "state" symbol to create a symbol pair "word".

Two symbols First symbol: What are we talking about? Second symbol: What is its current condition?
Three Choices Each pair can be a Thing (Noun), an Idea (Feeling), or an Action (Verb).
Use numbers for amounts, initials for names and ISO formats for dates and times. Create complex words by joining symbol pairs with a dot (.)
