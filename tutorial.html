The 15-Minute Masterclass

Estimated time: 15 minutes. Outcome: Fluency in Two Talk.

01. The Architecture

Two Talk is a Positional Language. Meaning is created by where a symbol sits in a pair.

[OBJECT] + [ACTION/IDEA] = MEANING

Think of it like Subject + Verb. If you see @ (Person) followed by : (Time), you are describing a person's time — an Attendee or Schedule.

02. The Core 16 Symbols

Learn these 16 categories. They are the "DNA" of every note.

@ Person
# Item/Tool
/ Method/Flow
: Time
^ Place/Context
" Message/Data
% Resource
* State/Quality
! Priority
? Unknown/Risk
> Outcome
< Issue
= Baseline
+ Addition
- Subtraction
\ Support

03. Doubling Up

When you repeat a symbol (e.g., @@), you are describing the Purest Form of that category.

@@ A specific human (Person).
## The primary instrument (Tool).
!! A critical situation (Emergency).
\\ The ultimate foundation (Support).

04. The "Action" Modifiers

The second symbol modifies the first. Look at how + (Add) and - (Remove) change things:

@+ Recruit / New Hire (Person + Add)
@- Resignation / Departure (Person - Remove)
%+ Revenue / Income (Resource + Add)
%- Expense / Cost (Resource - Remove)

05. Grammar & Linking

To write complex thoughts, use Actor → Action → Target. Link pairs with dots for compound modifiers.

Scenario: "The manager updated the project file."

@> "+ #%

(Leader) (Message-Update) (Item-File)
